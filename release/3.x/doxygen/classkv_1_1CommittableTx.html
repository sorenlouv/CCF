Commit this transaction to the local KV and submit it to consensus for replication

A transaction can either succeed and replicate (kv::CommitResult::SUCCESS), fail because of a conflict with other transactions (kv::CommitResult::FAIL_CONFLICT), or succeed locally, but fail to replicate (kv::CommitResult::FAIL_NO_REPLICATE).

Transactions that fail are rolled back, no matter the reason.
